Our Story

Our family moved into this property that was once a wheat farm, with the hopes to turn it into something more sustainable for the community. Instead of just focusing on the monoculture that degraded the land, we are growing a variety of fruits, vegtables and fungi that will restore nutrients to the soil. We are also keeping some fields of wild flowers to give a habitat to local pollinators and support a natural ecosytem to help us avoid using pesticides. Additionally, we have an Airbnb on the property to help support our mission and show people first hand what a sustainable organic farm can look like.
